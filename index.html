Strategic Frontline Briefing

FIELD MANUAL

Mission Objective

Seize enemy gold and bring it home.

Capture the opposing team's gold bars and return them safely to your side of the board while protecting your attackers.

2 Attackers
2 Defenders
3 Villagers
1 Team Captain

Core Rules

  • Any piece can move in any direction.
  • Rolling the die determines how many spaces you move this turn.
  • Each piece must keep moving in the same direction for the whole turn.
  • To capture a piece or take a gold bar, your piece must overlap that piece.
  • When any piece is captured, it leaves the game and does not return to its starting position.

Unit Roles

  • Attacker (A): Can pick up gold bars but cannot capture enemy pieces.
  • Defender (D): Can capture enemy pieces, cannot pick up gold, and cannot cross the middle line.
  • Villager (V): Can attack and defend, but cannot take gold.
  • Captain (C): A chosen villager that can both capture enemies and carry gold.

Gold & Captures

  • If an attacker or captain overlaps a gold bar, that piece picks up the gold.
  • Attackers cannot capture players. If they pass over an enemy piece, nothing happens.
  • Defenders and villagers capture every enemy piece they pass over in their path.
  • Defenders cannot pick up gold.

Victory Conditions

  • Win by getting all 4 gold bars from the other team.

Setup Notes

  • Players can place their own starting board before the game begins.
  • Each side must place 2 attackers, 2 defenders, 3 villagers, 1 captain, and 4 gold bars before the match begins. Team pieces stay on their own half, while gold bars can be placed on any open square.
  • The match starts only after both sides have placed all of their pieces.
